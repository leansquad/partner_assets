Marketing Questionnaire - Sam's Club

Congratulations on being selected to participate in the Sam's Club Extreme Value Program! This is an exciting opportunity to showcase your inventory in Sam's Club and SamsClub.com. The input on this form is necessary to create the artwork that will be printed on the pullcards and pallets that will be on display. Please be accurate with the information as we cannot be responsible for the actual verbiage or terms and conditions of your product. Also below you will be able to upload images and files needed for creating the artwork. You can find more information on the Asset Guidelines or if you have questions please contact your Account Manager with InComm.
* - Required Fields
i - More Information


Product Image Upload

A product image is required. The image area to fill is 10”w x ~5”h (including bleed) and minimum resolution of 300dpi at that size. Do not provide images that include text or other graphic elements unless it has been specifically designed to the image area dimensions.

Logo Upload

A high resolution logo is required A vector format is strongly preferred so we can scale your logo without loss of image quality (AI, EPS, PDF). If raster, the minimum resolution is 300dpi at final size (~16-20”h for the skirts).

Card/Certificate/Ticket Image Upload

Scan or take a photo of the front and back of your gift card, certificate or ticket/voucher. You may upload two separate files if needed. If you are submitting the front and back separately, Please name your files the following: Front of product: PartnerName_Front.file format Back of Product: PartnerName_Back.file format For example: My restaurant is Sweet Pea Cafe and my sample image files are jpegs. Sweet_Pea_Cafe_Front.jpg Sweet_Pea_Cafe_Back.jpg Please note - This is not in lieu of the hi-resolution product image shot needed for the pallet assets. See the Marketing Asset Guidelines for image requirements.
